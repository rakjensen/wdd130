The Thrill Seeker's Run

For adrenaline junkies! Tackle Class IV and V rapids on this intense half-day adventure. Brace yourself for narrow chutes, steep drops, and exhilarating twists.

Includes: Safety gear, expert guides, and a snack break

Family Float Adventure

Ideal for families or beginners, this trip provides a scenic, gentle ride through Class I and II rapids. Take in beautiful river views while splashing around in calmer waters.

Includes: Safety gear, guide, and a mid-trip picnic stop.

Overnight Wilderness Expedition

Spend two days rafting through Class III rapids and camping riverside under the stars. This all-inclusive experience offers a blend of adventure, nature, and relaxation.

Includes: All meals, camping gear, safety equipment, and professional guides.
